We need to classify different types of location so we can improve the quality of search results and better communicate them to applicants.

This is a first pass at trying to define the terms we should use.

Discussion is on the Google Document

Host school

A school where a student is based for most of their time. Also known as a parent school.

University campus

A university campus where a student will be based for a large part of their training, when not on school placements.

Administrative address

No training takes place here. Students do not need to go to this location. This might be the headquarters or registered address of your organisation.

Training centre

Students will spend some time training here, but it is not their main location. This can be a school.

Regional hub

It represents many locations in an area, students apply to a region. It is not a physical address that students visit.

School (other)

Catch-all?

University campus (accredited provider)

A university campus where a student will study for the PGCE parts of their course.
