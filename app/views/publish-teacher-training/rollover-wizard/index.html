{{ title }}

Following on from rollover reckons, this shows a full rollover wizard.

Steps are:

  • begin rollover, explain anything that’s new in this cycle
  • pick the courses to copy
  • pick the locations to copy
  • check your answers
  • confirm and redirect to a new top-level cycles page
  • two cycles now available: current and next

Copying courses

Previously we asked users to select the courses to copy. In research we found that most providers want to copy most, if not all, their courses. This design defaults to having every course checked.

If providers don’t copy any course, is that ok? They’ll need to add new ones.

Copying locations

We haven’t tested copying locations yet but have assumed a similar need to courses – most will stay the same.

Any location that isn’t copied needs to be removed from every course being copied.

Do providers need to copy at least one location?

Main site

While not yet shown, we might want to include a screen to rename ‘Main Site’ to something clearer.

On completion

At the end of the wizard a new cycle is created. Users are taken to a new top-level screen. For multi-org users it sits between the Organisations page and the Organisation:

One cycle (eg before rollover period):

  • Organisations
  • → Organisation showing courses, locations and about your or

Two cycles (eg during rollover period):

  • Organisations
  • → Organisation showing two cycles
  • → → Cycle showing courses, locations and about your org
